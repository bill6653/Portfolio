Who We Are!

A little about our team..

Image

PawSafe Health is a collaboration of the minds of the Jakes (yes, there are two of them!), Khi, Diana, Billy, and Shawn. We gathered together in the fall of 2022 from all across the country with one goal in mind. Yes, all of our passions pointed straight toward the heart of an issue that is dear to many hearts. That is, of course, making sure your furry friends receive the care they need when they need it!

What is PawSafe Health?

We were hoping you'd ask!


We know the hustle and bustle of daily life is difficult just taking care of yourself, and adding a family pet that needs a little extra help to the equation doesn't always make it easier! PawSafe Health can be your go-to when it comes to remembering what prescriptions to give your fur-babies, and the best times to be reminded. You'll be able to use PawSafe Health's handy scheduler to input the time, as well as the prescriptions and dosages so you don't forget.
